The Best Formula One Team

Lenny Martinez Dominguez

In the 69-year history of the sport, over 900 men and women have raced to win the Formula One Driver's Cup. Only 33 drivers have held it.

These 33 drivers drove for the same 16 teams.

But which team has had the best championship season?

To answer this question, I'll be comparing three different ways of defining "best":

  1. most wins in a season
  2. most podium spots in a season
  3. best average race finishes in a season
